A Violin/Piano Recital
Guest violinist Helen Chang Haertzen will team up with pianist Horacio Nuguid to welcome back audiences with a delightful selection of works for violin and piano. Familiar sonatas by Handel and Mozart will be included in the program as well as well-known and beloved melodies of Raff, Bernstein, Kreisler and Tchaikovsky.
